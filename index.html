Daniel Cooper Bermudez

Political scientist // Aspiring Programmer // Organizer

Daniel Cooper Bermudez is a political scientist, aspiring programmer, and translator currently based in Maracaibo, Venezuela. Daniel has also lived and worked in Philadelphia, PA and Immokalee, FL in the past few years. He is an active community member wherever he resides, always seeking to build relationships through shared callings and political organizing. He has worked with the Coalition of Immokalee Workers as a digital archivist and organizer, with Philadelphia Councilwoman María Quiñones Sánchez as an intern, and with Popular Resistance as a writer and on web content.
