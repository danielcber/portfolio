Daniel Cooper Bermudez

Political strategist // Rising Programmer // Translator

Daniel Cooper Bermúdez is a political strategist, rising programmer, and translator currently based in Maracaibo, Venezuela. Daniel has also lived and worked in Philadelphia, PA and Immokalee, FL. He is an active community member wherever he resides, always seeking to build relationships through shared callings and political organizing. Daniel currently works with the Zulia Comission for Human Rights (Codhez) as the Development Coordinator, developing and managing projects as well as building the organizations digital infrastructure. He has worked with the Coalition of Immokalee Workers as a digital archivist and organizer, with Philadelphia Councilwoman María Quiñones Sánchez as an intern, and with Popular Resistance as a writer and on web content.
